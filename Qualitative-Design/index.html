A Story of The MET Collection

Connecting Art through Time, Geography and Content.






The story above has been created by linking every item from the MET collection and randomly following a link. These links are between Time (when the artist was born, died or when the piece was created), Geography (where the artist was born, where the art was created or excavated) and Content (the subject of the artwork). If you would like to find out more you can visit the project on GitHub.
